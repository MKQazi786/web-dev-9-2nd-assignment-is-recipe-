Brain Masala Katakat

Ingredients Required

  • Brain Goat
    2 whole,250g

  • Onion
    1 Medium finely diced

  • Garlic Paste
    1 tablespoon

  • Ginger
    1 tablespoo fine strips/julienne

  • Green Chillies
    1-2 tablespoon,chopped

  • Cilantro/Fresh Coriander
    3 tablespoons chopped

  • Oil/Ghee
    3/4 cup/100ml

  • Butter
    2-3 tablespoon

  • Special Brain Masala
    • 2 tablespoons

STEPS OF COOKING

  1. Boil the brain in water for 10 minutes and discard the water. Remove top lining on the brain and chop it into small pieces.
  2. Heat oil/ghee and fry the onion until light golden. Add garlic,ginger,green chillies and Speacial Brain Masala mixed in 2 tablespoons of water.Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the boiled brain and stir-fry continuously for about 5-7 minutes
  4. Add green coriander,butter and stir. Serve with naans
